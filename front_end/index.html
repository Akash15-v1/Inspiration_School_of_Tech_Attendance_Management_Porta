About the Attendance Portal

The Student Attendance Management Portal is a digital platform designed to simplify attendance tracking in educational institutions. It provides students with instant access to their attendance records and allows faculty members to record and manage attendance efficiently.

The portal offers a centralized system that improves accuracy, reduces paperwork, saves time, and enables better monitoring of student attendance through a modern web-based interface.

Key Features

  • Student Login
  • Faculty Login
  • Attendance Management
  • Attendance Reports
  • Profile Management
  • Cloud-Based Architecture
  • Secure Authentication
